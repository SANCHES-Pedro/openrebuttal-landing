FAQ

Does it work for all OpenReview conferences?

Yes—NeurIPS, ICLR, ICML, etc. If some reviews are private, you’ll need to authenticate. We handle tokens securely.

How accurate are the AI rebuttals?

They’re starting points—concise and context-aware. You still refine the tone, add references, or push back strategically.

Can I customize classification labels?

Absolutely. Add your own taxonomy—“Needs new experiment,” “Typos,” “Minor wording,” etc.

Do I need my own OpenAI key?

You can use ours or plug in your own—your choice. (Bring-your-own-key is on the roadmap if not already live.)

Can multiple authors edit at once?

Yes—push to Google Sheets or share the CSV/Excel. Assign owners, change statuses, and iterate fast.
